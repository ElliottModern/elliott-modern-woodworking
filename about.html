ABOUT

"I founded Elliott Modern to combine my love of building and my artistic eye for custom design. This passion drives me to create unique, innovative designs that showcase both my technical expertise and creative vision."

-Kevin Elliott

My attention to detail has led me to pursue a diverse range of opportunities in which I could develop and hone my skills. My career has taken me from designing custom spaces for the restaurant and hospitality industries, to implementing 3D design software for a custom millwork production shop, and currently to designing specialty vehicles that serve as mobile communication command centers.

Through these experiences, I have discovered my true passion:

Building and Custom Design.

As a mechanical engineer, I have always recognized the importance of materials in my designs. I have had the opportunity to work with a wide range of materials, including metals, woods, and composites. However, it is wood that has particularly captured my attention and inspired me to focus on woodworking.

I find that wood adds a warmth and welcoming touch to a design that cannot be achieved with cold, hard materials such as metal and concrete. Additionally, the natural grain and color variations in wood allow me to incorporate a touch of the organic and wild into my modern, straight-lined and angular designs. Overall, I believe that the contrast between the modern and the natural elements creates a unique and striking aesthetic in my work.
