WELCOME

Welcome to Elliott Modern, a small woodworking shop specializing in handmade cutting boards and other modern home goods.

My name is Kevin Elliott and I am the owner and sole employee of Elliott Modern. With a passion for woodworking and a commitment to sustainability, I started this business with the goal of creating functional and beautiful pieces that enhance the home.

At Elliott Modern, every piece is made with care and attention to detail. From selecting the finest woods to sanding and finishing each item by hand, I take pride in delivering top-quality products to my customers.

In addition to cutting boards, I offer a variety of other items such as serving trays, cheese boards, and custom furniture. Please take a look at my portfolio to see some of my past work, and don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or would like to place an order.

Thank you for considering Elliott Modern. I look forward to working with you!
